HOW TO USE YOUR CREDIT CARD
===========================

1. VIEWING CARD INFO:
   - Use the "Credit_Card" item from your inventory
   - Shows: Balance, Limit, Available Credit, Interest Rate

2. USING FOR PURCHASES:
   - The credit card works automatically in shops (if integrated)
   - Payment priority: Wallet -> Bank -> Credit Card
   - Charges are added to your card balance (not deducted from wallet/bank)

3. MAKING PAYMENTS:
   - Go to ATM or Tablet App -> Credit & Loans
   - Enter payment amount in "Pay Card" field
   - Click "Pay Card" button
   - Payments improve your credit score

4. CREDIT CARD DETAILS:
   - Credit Limit: $5,000
   - Interest Rate: Based on credit score (12-25%)
   - Interest accrues every 2 hours on outstanding balances
   - Carrying balance decreases credit score

5. IMPORTANT:
   - You must have the "Credit_Card" item in inventory to use it
   - Your account must be active (credit_hasCard = true)
   - Available credit = Limit - Current Balance
   - Make regular payments to avoid interest and maintain good credit
